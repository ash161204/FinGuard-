FinGuard Vocabulary Simplification Plan

**1. GENERAL TERMINOLOGY**
- "Corpus" -> "Total Savings" or "Nest Egg"
- "XIRR" -> "Average Annual Return"
- "EMI" -> "Monthly Loan Payments"
- "SIP" -> "Regular Monthly Investment"
- "Regime" -> "Tax System" (e.g., "Old vs New Tax System")
- "Extraction" -> "Reading your document"
- "Normalization" -> "Cleaning up data"

**2. HOME SCREEN (PULSE)**
- "Money Health Score" -> "Financial Fitness Score"
- "Insights" -> "Smart Tips" or "Suggestions"
- "Actions" -> "To-Do List"
- "Inferred Actions" -> "Automated Recommendations"

**3. TAX WIZARD (FORM 16)**
- "Section 80C/80D" -> "Tax-Saving Investments" and "Health Insurance Tax Break"
- "HRA received" -> "House Rent Allowance"
- "LTA" -> "Travel Allowance"
- "Tax Payable" -> "Total Tax You Owe"
- "Refund / Payable" -> "Final Tax Balance (Refund if negative)"
- "Professional Tax" -> "Employment Tax"

**4. MUTUAL FUND X-RAY (CAMS)**
- "Portfolio XIRR" -> "Your Overall Growth Rate"
- "Risk Profile" -> "Investment Risk Level"
- "Portfolio Hygiene" -> "Account Health Checks"
- "Rebalancing" -> "Adjusting your investment mix"
- "Holdings" -> "Your Investments"
- "Direct/Regular Plan" -> "Low-fee (Direct) vs Commission-based (Regular)"

**5. FIRE PLANNER**
- "FIRE Planner" -> "Early Retirement Planner"
- "Current Corpus" -> "Current Total Savings"
- "Required Corpus" -> "Goal Savings for Retirement"
- "Projected Corpus" -> "Estimated Savings at Retirement"
- "Inflation" -> "Cost of Living Increase"
- "Return Rate" -> "Expected Growth Rate"
- "Gap" -> "Shortfall" or "Remaining to Goal"

**6. PROFILE & INPUTS**
- "Monthly Cash Flow" -> "Monthly Money In & Out"
- "Emergency Fund" -> "Rainy Day Fund"
- "Term Life Cover" -> "Life Insurance Amount"
- "Health Insurance Cover" -> "Medical Insurance Amount"

**7. USER EXPLANATIONS (TOOLTIPS/HELPERS)**
- Instead of "Express as decimal (0.12)", use "Enter percentage (e.g., 12%)" and handle the math in the background.
- Add "Why this matters?" small text for complex fields like 80CCD1B (NPS).
